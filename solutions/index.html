Settlement, Issuance, and Liquidity for AI Agent Economies

UUSD is the native settlement layer for autonomous software, designed so that humans, AI agents, and institutions can transact on the same programmable dollar rail. The platform is organized around three pillars.

Programmable Settlement

UUSD is an ERC-20 dollar with EIP-2612 Permit, EIP-3009 TransferWithAuthorization, and batch transfer primitives. Software systems can authorize, move, and reconcile value without custodial intermediaries or human-scale payment latency.

  • Gasless authorization flows for agent-operated wallets
  • Batch transfers up to 200 recipients per transaction
  • Deterministic CREATE2 address across every supported EVM chain

Institutional Issuance

Anything Labs partners with institutions to deploy compliant, upgradeable on-chain dollar products on shared UUSD infrastructure — pausable controls, multisig custody, and audited contract standards.

Yield-Bearing Liquidity

yUUSD is a yield-bearing, non-custodial representation of UUSD with a target-APY model and associated LP Pool, giving counterparties access to on-chain dollar liquidity without leaving the UUSD infrastructure perimeter.
