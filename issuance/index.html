Launch Stablecoins on UUSD Infrastructure

UUSD provides the contract standards, deployment tooling, and governance patterns required to launch institutional-grade on-chain dollar products. Anything Labs partners directly with issuing institutions to deploy and operate compliant stablecoins on shared infrastructure.

What Issuance on UUSD Includes

  • Audited contract codebase, reviewed by SlowMist
  • TransparentUpgradeableProxy pattern for safe, governed upgrades
  • Separation of Owner, Admin, and Minter roles with least-privilege controls
  • Deterministic CREATE2 deployment for consistent multi-chain addresses
  • Safe multisig custody of Owner and ProxyAdmin keys

Contract Standards

  • ERC-20 with 18 decimals
  • EIP-2612 Permit for gasless approvals
  • EIP-3009 TransferWithAuthorization for authorized off-chain signed transfers
  • Batch transfers — up to 200 recipients per transaction
  • Freeze/unfreeze at the account level for compliance controls
  • Global pause/unpause for emergency response
  • Ownable2Step for safer ownership transitions

Who This Is For

Financial institutions, exchanges, regulated fintechs, and infrastructure partners seeking to deploy on-chain dollar products without rebuilding core stablecoin contracts, governance, or operational tooling.
